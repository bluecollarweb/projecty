1.  Digital Calculator


A Digital Calculator is a exciting and interesting project for new developer and programmers for testing their skills and knowledge.

2.  Rock Paper Scissor


A Rock Scissor Paper game is a exciting and interesting project for new developer and programmers for testing their skills and knowledge and for refreshing old childhood memories

3.  Number Guessing


A number guessing game is a simple guessing game where a user is supposed to guess a number between 0 and N in a maximum of 10 attempts. The game will end after 10 attempts and if the player failed to guess the number, and then he loses the game. Examples: N = 100.

Time Duration : 6 to 15 hours



4.  Music player


Make a software that plays audio files encoded in MP3 and other audio formats. On the software side, applications that reside in the user's computer, such as iTunes, Windows Media Player and RealPlayer, are used to organize a music collection, play audio files and rip music from a CD.

Time Duration : 20 to 28 hours
